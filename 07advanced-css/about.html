A Brief History

In 2019, Caleb and Joe were just a couple of friends and students in the UVU graphic design program . Back then, they couldn't help but notice that the design scene in Utah was missing something that truly spoke to them. So, they took matters into their own hands and launched Equity Business Solutions. What began as a small-scale operation has since blossomed into a successful Provo-based business, and they've expanded into a tight-knit team of four, proudly serving the whole of northern Utah from their downtown Provo hub.

Core Values

Creativity
• We aim to foster innovation and artistic expression. We want imaginative thinking to be at the heart of everything we do. We encourage our team to explore uncharted territory and push the boundaries of design.
Quality
• We are dedicated to excellence in every project, ensuring that each design we create is of the highest standard. We take great pride in our attention to detail and unwavering commitment to delivering the best work possible.
Collaboration
• At Equity Business Solutions, We strive to embrace teamwork and open communication. We believe in working closely with our clients and staff to understand their visions and bring them to life. We value input from our clients and staff to help create designs that succeed and resonate.
Integrity
• We uphold honesty and ethical conduct in all our interactions, we prioritize transparency and integrity in our business. We believe that trust is the foundation of lucrative and enduring client relationships.
Adaptability
• Here at Equity Business Solutions, we recognize the ever-evolving nature of design. We remain flexible and open to change, staying current with industry trends and emerging technologies. We aim to ensure we can meet the dynamic needs of our clients and provide forward-thinking solutions.
